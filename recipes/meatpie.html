Meat Pie

Description

Every Thanksgiving Dinner, my family always has a delicious meat pie as apart of the meal. It is always one of the foods I most look forward to each year. This delicious meat pie that we will be creating will be curated from the recipe that my mother has taught me to make. You will surely enjoy this delicious treat, or meal!

Ingredients

    Pie crust
    1 lb of ground beef
    quarter lb of ground pork
    large potatoes
    1 sleeve of Ritz Crackers
    McCormick chicken seasoning
