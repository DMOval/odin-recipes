Meat Pie

Description

Every Thanksgiving Dinner, my family always has a delicious meat pie as apart of the meal. It is always one of the foods I most look forward to each year. This delicious meat pie that we will be creating will be curated from the recipe that my mother has taught me to make. You will surely enjoy this delicious treat, or meal!

Ingredients

    Pie crust
    1 lb of ground beef
    quarter lb of ground pork
    large potatoes
    1 sleeve of Ritz Crackers
    McCormick chicken seasoning

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit
  • Grab pie pan
  • Roll out pie crust into pan
  • Boil potatoes
  • begin cooking beef, once its mostly cooked throw in the pork and cook until done
  • strain and mash potatoes by hand
  • place sleeve of ritz in a plastic baggy and crush them finely
  • combine crackers, potatoes, and meat
  • put meat filling into laid out pie crust
  • cover and score the top of the pie
  • place pie into oven and cook for 45 minutes
  • take out of oven and let cool, or enjoy hot! Use gravy or ketchup for best results
